Lasagna Recipe

Discription:

A baked dish of flat pasta, marinara sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Herbs
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella

Steps:

  1. Lightly cook pasta
  2. Combine herbs and ricotta
  3. Layer sauce ricotta and nodles
  4. Top with mozzarella
  5. Bake at 350 until cheese is browned
