Marinara Sauce Recipe

Discription:

An Italian tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Oil the sauce pan and add crushed peper flakes
  2. After a few minutes add onion
  3. Add garlic
  4. Add diced tomatos
  5. Simmer 20 minutes
  6. Add basil an salt to taste
