Pizza Recipe

Discription:

A perfect bread sauce and cheese combination.

Ingredients:

  • Flower
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Olive Oil
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella

Steps:

  1. Combine flower, yeast, water, and a pinch of sugar
  2. Wait until the dough has doubled in size
  3. Roll dough out into a disk
  4. Put sauce and cheese on dough
  5. Bake at 425 until golden brown
